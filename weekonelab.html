Cambodia

Cambodia is a country located in the southern portion of the Indochinese Peninsula in Southeast Asia, spanning an area of 181,035 square kilometres, bordered by Thailand to the northwest, Laos to the north, Vietnam to the east, and the Gulf of Thailand to the southwest. Scr. Wikipedia

Things to see in Cambodia

  • Tuol Sleng Prison Museum
  • Angkor National Museum
  • Angkor Wat
  • Royal Palace, Phnom Penh
  • National Museum of Cambodia
  • Ta Dumbong Statue
